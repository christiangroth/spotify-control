Playback Settings

Playback Data

Rebuild all processed playback data (app_playback and app_track_data) from scratch by reprocessing the raw Spotify data. Use this if your playback stats appear incomplete or outdated.

Artist Playback Processing

Control which artists are included in playback processing. Setting an artist to inactive removes all their playback data. Reactivating an artist triggers a full playback data rebuild.
